About/Us

Discover Our/Berlin Vodka, a fusion of artistry and urban essence. Rooted in Berlin's heart, our distillery embodies the city's creativity and spirit. Using local ingredients and time-honored techniques, each bottle reflects Berlin's dynamic energy. From artists to entrepreneurs, we salute those redefining traditions. Join us in toasting the unity of diverse spirits and the vibrant soul of Our/Berlin Vodka.

Since 1934, we've passionately crafted premium vodka at our Berlin distillery. Our legacy marries tradition and innovation, resulting in a vodka that captures the essence of Berlin's spirit.
